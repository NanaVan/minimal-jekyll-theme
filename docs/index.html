Introducing Minimal

After months of slaving away at my Macbook, i’m so glad to finally reveal my first open source theme to the world. Woohoo

This is Minimal Jekyll Theme and it looks so damn good if i do say so myself. Lets take a look at what you can achieve starting with the most minimal of layouts.

  • A minimal post with a title
  • A post with a title and subtitle
  • A post with post meta above title
  • A post with post meta and an author bio
  • A post with share icons.
  • A post with categories and tags
  • A post with comments turned off
  • This post has been updated

You can find out more info about customizing the Minimal Jekyll theme in the theme documentation, as well as basic Jekyll usage documentation at jekyllrb.com.
